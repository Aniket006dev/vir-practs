What's Vir-practs ?

Virtual Practicals is a web-based platform designed to modernize how students complete and submit computer science lab assignments. It replaces traditional paper-based methods with a streamlined online system, ensuring a more efficient and accessible learning experience.

Why we ?

With Virtual Practicals, students can work on their assignments anytime, anywhere, without depending on physical labs. This flexibility helps students manage their time effectively while fostering a more inclusive approach to education.

Benefits

The platform simplifies the assignment process for students and reduces administrative efforts for instructors. Its organized structure supports seamless submission and evaluation, enhancing productivity for all users.

Innovation

Virtual Practicals integrates technology into academics, bridging the gap between traditional learning and digital transformation. It equips students and institutions with tools for a future-ready, tech-savvy educational environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Virtual Practicals?

Virtual Practicals is an online platform that helps students perform coding lab assignments and practicals digitally.

How does Virtual Practicals work?

Students log in, select their subject, and perform assignments online. The system auto-evaluates and provides feedback.

Is Virtual Practicals free to use?

Yes, the platform is free for students and educators. Additional features may be available in premium versions.

Can I track my progress?

Yes, users can track their completed assignments, scores, and improvement over time.

Which programming languages are supported?

Virtual Practicals currently supports Python, C, C++, and Java, with more languages coming soon.
